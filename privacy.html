Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We may collect personal details such as your name, phone number, email address, pet details, and appointment preferences when you use our online forms or book services.

How We Use Your Information

  • To schedule appointments and communicate with you.
  • To provide a better grooming experience tailored to your pet.
  • To send booking confirmations, reminders, and promotional content (only if subscribed).

Information Security

We implement appropriate measures to safeguard your personal data and ensure it is not lost, misused, or accessed by unauthorized individuals.

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except when legally required or if necessary to provide our services.

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. Please contact us directly to make such requests.
